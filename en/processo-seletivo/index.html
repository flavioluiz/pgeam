üéØ Selection Process Stages

The PG-EAM selection process consists of multiple evaluation stages:

1. Mandatory Exams

‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT: All applicants must take a logic and basic mathematics exam, unless they have passed this exam since 2022.

For Master’s and PMG candidates:

  • Basic mathematics exam (mandatory)
  • English proficiency exam (mandatory)

For Doctoral candidates:

  • Basic mathematics exam (mandatory)
  • English proficiency certificate (TOEFL, IELTS, TOEIC, etc.) - required at enrollment

2. Documentation and Academic Analysis

  • Academic transcripts
  • Updated CV/resume (Lattes curriculum for Brazilian candidates)
  • Preliminary research proposal
  • Diplomas (bachelor’s for master’s; master’s for doctorate)

3. Advisor Selection

  • Identification of faculty advisor compatible with research topic
  • Approval by intended advisor

4. Program Council Evaluation

  • Final analysis of all documentation
  • Collegial decision on admission

Prior Contact with Faculty

PG-EAM strongly recommends that candidates contact in advance faculty members accredited to the program with whom they identify regarding research topics. Before making contact, it is highly recommended that candidates check the professor’s detailed profile on the website (previous publications, Lattes curriculum, etc.) to verify if there is a real affinity and alignment of interests.

üìö General Requirements

For Master’s Degree

  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or related fields
  • Good academic performance
  • English proficiency
  • Alignment with program research lines

For Doctoral Degree

  • Master’s degree in Engineering or related fields
  • Research track record (publications, projects)
  • Advanced English proficiency
  • Research proposal aligned with the program

Military Personnel

Brazilian military (Air Force, Army, or Navy) must follow specific procedures from the Graduate Program in Operational Applications (PPGAO).

Foreign military personnel must submit their application through their country’s embassy to the Brazilian Air Force General Staff, which will coordinate with PPGAO.

More information: üîó www.ppgao.ita.br (in Portuguese)

Civil Servants of the Air Force Command

Civil servants must obtain prior authorization through the Administrative Subdepartment (SDA) of DCTA.
