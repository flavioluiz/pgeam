Research

The Graduate Program in Aeronautical and Mechanical Engineering (PG-EAM) at the Instituto Tecnológico de Aeronáutica (ITA) conducts research in various areas of engineering.

Research Areas

Aeronautical and Mechanical Engineering 1 (EAM1)

  • Aerodynamics
  • Propulsion
  • Aeronautical Structures

Aeronautical and Mechanical Engineering 2 (EAM2)

  • Materials and Manufacturing Processes
  • Aerospace Systems
  • Control and Automation

Aeronautical and Mechanical Engineering 3 (EAM3)

  • Orbital Mechanics and Control
  • Space Systems
  • Space Technology

Research Projects

Research projects are developed in partnership with aerospace companies and national and international research institutions.

Laboratories

PG-EAM has modern laboratories equipped for cutting-edge research in the aerospace field.

Research Groups

Our research groups work on:

  • Computational fluid dynamics
  • Composite materials
  • Satellite technology
  • Unmanned aerial vehicles
  • Aerospace propulsion
  • Structural analysis

Collaboration

We maintain active collaborations with:

  • Brazilian Space Agency (AEB)
  • National Institute for Space Research (INPE)
  • Embraer
  • Airbus
  • International universities
