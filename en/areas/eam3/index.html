EAM3 - Materials, Manufacturing and Automation

Aeronautical and Mechanical Engineering 3 area details

Overview

The EAM3 area encompasses materials science, manufacturing processes, and automation technologies, focusing on advanced materials and Industry 4.0 applications.

Research Areas

Materials Science

  • Metallic materials
  • Ceramic materials
  • Polymer composites
  • Nanomaterials

Manufacturing Processes

  • Machining processes
  • Casting and molding
  • Welding technologies
  • Surface engineering

Materials Processing and Characterization

  • Heat treatment
  • Microstructural analysis
  • Mechanical testing
  • Non-destructive testing

Advanced Materials

  • Smart materials
  • Bio-materials
  • Aerospace materials
  • High-performance alloys

Mechatronics

  • Control systems
  • Sensors and actuators
  • Embedded systems
  • Robotics

Industrial Automation

  • PLC programming
  • SCADA systems
  • Process control
  • Quality control

Advanced Manufacturing and Industry 4.0

  • Additive manufacturing
  • Digital manufacturing
  • IoT in manufacturing
  • Artificial intelligence applications
